Caloric Surplus Fundamentals

Key Principle: Consume 300-500 calories above your maintenance level

  • Clean bulk: 200-300 calorie surplus
  • Dirty bulk: 500+ calorie surplus
  • Monitor weight gain: 0.5-1 lb per week

Macronutrient Distribution

Protein: 1.6-2.2g per kg body weight
Carbs: 4-7g per kg body weight
Fats: 0.8-1.2g per kg body weight

Recovery Techniques

Originates in the nervous system. Managed through adequate sleep and stress management.

Occurs in the muscles themselves. Addressed through proper nutrition and recovery protocols.
